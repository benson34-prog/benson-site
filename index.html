KENYATTA UNIVERSITY

Kenyatta University (KU) is one of Kenya’s leading public universities, known for its commitment to excellence in education, research, and innovation. Located in Kahawa, just 20 kilometers from Nairobi's city center, the university sits on a vast, serene campus along Thika Superhighway. It was officially established as a university in 1985 through an Act of Parliament, although its origins date back to 1965 when it was founded as Kenyatta College.

Named after Kenya’s first President, Mzee Jomo Kenyatta, the university has grown significantly over the years in terms of infrastructure, academic programs, and student population. It offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and certificate courses across diverse disciplines including Education, Medicine, Engineering, Law, Economics, Business, Arts, Environmental Studies, and ICT. The institution is organized into several schools such as the School of Engineering, School of Law, School of Medicine, School of Business, and many others, each focusing on developing specialized knowledge and professional skills.

Kenyatta University prides itself on being a center of academic excellence with a strong emphasis on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship. It has made significant strides in developing modern learning facilities including digital libraries, science labs, innovation hubs, and e-learning platforms. Additionally, KU has embraced internationalization through academic collaborations, student and faculty exchange programs, and hosting international conferences.

The university also boasts a vibrant student life, with numerous clubs, societies, sports teams, and cultural activities that nurture leadership, talent, and holistic development. Its emphasis on discipline, diversity, and community engagement has helped shape responsible citizens and professionals. Kenyatta University continues to play a pivotal role in the advancement of Kenya’s educational goals and remains a respected institution in Africa and beyond. Its vision is to be a dynamic, inclusive, and globally competitive center of excellence in teaching, learning, research, and service to humanity.
